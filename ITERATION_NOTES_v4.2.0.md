# Alert, Topic and Organization Intelligence

ParleyMap v4 adds the product layer that makes the site more than a travel map.

## Alerts

The alert model watches for public-source changes:

- New attendee announced.
- Agenda published.
- Speaker added.
- Event moved.
- Registration opens.

Alerts are source-watch items. They do not imply private travel or live location.

## Topic extraction

The interface now groups public records by recurring themes:

- AI.
- Defense.
- Inflation.
- Energy.
- China.
- Semiconductors.
- Climate.
- Trade.
- Ukraine.
- Middle East.
- Debt.
- Health.

Topic cards show current mentions, near-term movement and people connected to the theme in the current public archive.

## Recurring attendees

Recurring attendee rankings compare people across event families such as Davos, Bilderberg, Munich Security, FII, G7, G20, Jackson Hole, UNGA, COP, IMF / World Bank, BIS, OECD, OPEC, Milken and Sun Valley.

## Organization profiles

Organization cards currently cover BlackRock, NATO, IMF, BIS, Microsoft, OpenAI, Brookings, CFR, World Bank, OECD, OPEC, WEF, Group of Thirty, Bilderberg, Blackstone, JPMorgan, Federal Reserve and ECB.

## Event overlap matrix

The overlap matrix compares attendee overlap between major event families. It is designed to expose cross-network people and organizations.

## Organization penetration index

The penetration index ranks organizations by presence across high-signal event families.

## Topic migration

Topic migration shows how themes move across public forums and cities over time.

## Influence timeline

The horizontal timeline shows the yearly rhythm of public influence events.

## Power geography

Power geography turns key cities into clickable intelligence clusters: Zurich, Geneva, Basel, Washington, Brussels, London, Riyadh, Abu Dhabi, Beijing, New York, Davos, Munich, Paris, Vienna and Doha.
