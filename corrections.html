Corrections, takedown and image-rights review

Contact

Send correction, removal, privacy, safety, image-rights and advertising matters to torsten.sauter@icloud.com.

What to include

Include the page URL, the specific record or image, the issue, supporting source material, and whether the request concerns accuracy, safety, privacy, copyright, attribution, image rights, advertising or another matter.

Review handling

Records may be hidden during review if there is a credible safety, privacy, source-quality or rights concern. Outcomes may include correction, source downgrade, clearer wording, image replacement, record removal or no change with a documented reason.

Publication boundary

ParleyMap does not accept live tracking, private addresses, hotel guesses, private family movements, leaked itineraries, aircraft tracking, medical locations or unsourced sightings.
