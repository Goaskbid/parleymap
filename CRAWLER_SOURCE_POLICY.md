# Compliance and Safety Notes

## Core principle

Track public appearances, not people in real time.

## Allowed record types

- Official public meetings.
- Speeches.
- Press conferences.
- Public ceremonial appearances.
- Summits and event-program appearances.
- Official visits.
- Announced future public engagements.
- Post-event public records with source packs.

## Disallowed record types

- Private residences or home addresses.
- Hotels and temporary stays.
- Hospitals and medical visits without legal review and strong public-interest basis.
- Schools or child-focused precise locations unless officially public and safe.
- Leaked itineraries.
- Unofficial sightings.
- Real-time tracking.
- Inferred routes, live aircraft/convoy data, or security-sensitive timing.
- Doxxing, harassment, or stalking-enabling records.

## Risk tiers

- Tier 1: low operational risk, still public-source only.
- Tier 2: prominent figure with moderate safety/reputation concerns.
- Tier 3: heads of state, heads of government, senior ministers, royals, or high-risk public figures. Require conservative precision and stronger source packs.

## Publication rule

When in doubt, reduce precision, add uncertainty, or hold for review.
