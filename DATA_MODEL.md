# Crawler Source Policy

ParleyMap uses public, legally obtainable information only.

## Source tiers

**Verified** means an official source, host-event page, royal household, government calendar, multilateral organization, company event page, or public organizer page confirms the event.

**Likely** means multiple credible public sources describe the same public event, but the primary source still needs review.

**Reported** means a credible discovery source has created a lead. Reported items stay in the publication queue and do not become mapped public records without a stronger source pack.

## Future events

Future travel may be shown only when publicly announced by an official organizer, government, corporation, royal household, multilateral organization, venue, or public event organizer. The default precision is city-level.

## No-go categories

No private addresses, hotels, private homes, hospitals, school addresses, leaked itineraries, aircraft tracks, phone location data, motorcade detail, live proximity, security-detail information, or unsourced sightings.
