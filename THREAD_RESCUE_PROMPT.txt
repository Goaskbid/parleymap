The ParleyMap repo had three intertwined failures:
1. index.html could be crawler-visible as a one-line manifest, while the real app data lived in embedded demo-data or data/demo.json.
2. data/demo.json contained duplicate visible profile rows, especially Rafael Grossi, and fake dated source-watch cards such as IAEA nuclear diplomacy watch and City of London finance diplomacy watch.
3. AdSense was not crawler-visible because ads.txt and raw ad markers could be missing from the public surface.

The final architecture package treats index.html demo-data as source of truth when present, restores a safe full shell from git history when index.html is thin, repairs the canonical dataset globally, writes back only the demo-data block, mirrors to data/demo.json, preserves AdSense IDs from current files or git history, and writes hard audit reports.

Key files:
- scripts/parleymap-canonical-rescue.cjs
- scripts/parleymap-safe-roster-review.cjs
- data/curated-anchors.json
- data/official-event-seeds.json
- data/source-registry.json
- .github/workflows/data-validate.yml
- .github/workflows/nightly-refresh.yml
- .github/workflows/monthly-roster-review.yml
