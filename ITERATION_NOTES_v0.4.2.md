# Iteration notes v0.4.2

## Changes in this iteration

- Movement paths now render as colored segments rather than one plain line per person.
- Recent travel segments use stronger opacity and weight; historical segments recede; future tracks are dashed gold.
- Event explainers are docked in the card column, not over the map surface.
- Added a real top-200 roster seed list including Trump, Xi, Putin, Musk, Jaishankar, Gates, institutional heads, royalty, former leaders, finance, technology, sport, health, and diplomacy.
- Added direct portrait candidates for the first four requested names and browser-side Wikimedia/Wikipedia thumbnail hydration for the rest.
- Added validation checks for roster completeness and top-four portrait fields.

## Guardrail

The mapped event records remain synthetic. Real-person travel trails must not be published until source packs, public-interest criteria, precision rules, and visual/licensing audits are complete.
