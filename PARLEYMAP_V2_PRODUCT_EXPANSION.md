# Market Impact and Signals

## Market relevance framing

ParleyMap may show potential market relevance. It must never imply causation between a public appearance and a financial outcome unless a source explicitly supports that claim.

Each event can include:

- Relevant sectors.
- Relevant countries.
- Potentially exposed listed companies or institutions.
- Confidence level.
- Explanation of why the event might matter.

## Confidence labels

- High: official agenda or participant list directly references the sector or company.
- Medium: agenda and participant mix make the sector relevance plausible.
- Low: sector relevance is broad, contextual, or exploratory.

## Signal types

- New strategic relationship detected.
- High-profile meeting announced.
- First meeting in X years.
- Major summit cluster approaching.
- Diplomatic hotspot forming.
- Corporate executive delegation detected.
- Peace-process movement detected.
- Strategic industry concentration detected.

## User subscriptions

Users should be able to subscribe by person, country, city, organization, industry, topic, event type, relationship edge, or market sector.

## Institutional workflow

Signals should support audit logs, source trails, delivery status, deduplication, priority scoring, and analyst comments.
