# Granular crawler expansion: v3.2.0

The crawler should work in layers:

1. Official calendars and diaries for confirmed public engagements.
2. Official press rooms and leader-call readouts for meetings, calls and bilateral readouts.
3. Summit host pages for future windows, delegate lists, programmes, communiques and public attendance pages.
4. Corporate public-event pages for executive appearances, investor days, major conferences and keynote programmes.
5. Discovery layers such as public news indexes only as leads. A news item cannot publish a location record by itself unless the editorial policy explicitly marks it as reported rather than verified.

For less-documented names, the crawler should search official domains by person aliases, role title, organization, counterpart names, public event titles and verbs such as: met, received, hosted, opened, attended, addressed, spoke with, call, telephone conversation, readout, summit, forum, conference, delegation and visit.

Publication remains source-gated: no private address, hotel guess, live movement, leaked itinerary, aircraft tracking, residence inference or security-sensitive stop.
