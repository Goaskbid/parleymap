# Speech and University Crawler v3.4.0

The next crawler source family is official speech pages and public university / policy forum appearances.

## Source targets

- Federal Reserve Board speeches.
- ECB speeches and ECB Forum pages.
- Bank of England speeches.
- Bank of Japan speeches.
- Bank of Canada speeches and media advisories.
- BIS management speeches.
- University-hosted public policy forums.

## Publish rule

A speech card can publish if the page contains a public person name, date, city or host institution, and the source is official or host-public. The map shows only city-level anchors. A speech does not imply private travel routes or private meetings.

## Value

Speeches are usually the best available public signal for central bankers and institutional figures because they show where policy language is set, not merely where someone appeared.
