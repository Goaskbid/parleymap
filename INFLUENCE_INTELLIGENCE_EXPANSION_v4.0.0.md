# ParleyMap v4.0.0 — Alerts, Topics, Organizations and Event Graph

The accepted v3.9 map-first layout remains anchored. This iteration adds below-map intelligence modules:

- Build alerts: attendee announcements, agenda publication, speaker additions, event moves and registration windows.
- Topic radar: fastest-growing topics and most-discussed themes by year.
- Recurring attendees: most connected people, rising attendees and cross-network individuals.
- Organization profiles: BlackRock, NATO, IMF, BIS, Microsoft, OpenAI, Brookings, CFR and other institutions.
- Event overlap matrix: shared attendance across recurring venues.
- Organization penetration index: institution presence across the major event families.
- Topic migration: shows how narratives move across events.
- Influence timeline and power geography: annual event sequence and city-level influence hubs.

Alerts are not private tracking. They are source-watch items derived from public agendas, official pages, speaker lists, institutional calendars, readouts and event programmes.
