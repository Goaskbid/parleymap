# Brand and URL Notes

## Working name: ParleyMap

Rationale:

- "Parley" signals diplomacy, meetings, negotiation, and high-level conversation.
- "Map" makes the core interaction immediately obvious.
- The name is less surveillance-coded than names built around tracking or tracing.
- It works for politicians, royals, international bodies, business leaders, foundations, and sport governance.

## Tagline options

- Public power, placed in context.
- Where public power shows up.
- The sourced atlas of public appearances.
- Meetings, movements, and public records.
